Conference Schedule (Tentative)

Presentations of full papers (F-ID) are scheduled for 15 minutes, including 12 minutes for the presentation and 3 minutes for discussion.
Presentations of short papers (S-ID) are scheduled for 10 minutes, including 8 minutes for the presentation and 2 minutes for discussion.
All sessions take place at the Camera di Commercio di Modena – Sala Leonelli. Session chairs are responsible for keeping time and moderating the discussion.
